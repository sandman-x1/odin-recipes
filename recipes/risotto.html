Risotto

Description

Risotto is like a clingy baby. You can’t put it down, you can’t walk away from it, and you can’t ignore it. Its needs are simple, it just wants all of you. And if you give it all of your patient attention, it will turn into a puddle of love.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt, plus more
  • 6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 large white onion, chopped
  • 2 cups carnaroli, arborio, or sushi rice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut in pieces
  • 1 3/4 cups finely grated Parmesan
  • freshly ground black pepper
